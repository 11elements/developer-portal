Style guide

This page acts as a style guide for the Mozilla Developer Portal platform.

It details the components used throughout, ranging from small building blocks (atoms) through to larger page modules (organisms). The grouping of these is heavily inspired by Brad Frost's Atomic Design.

Atoms

Atoms are the basic building blocks within the platform. Within the platform these are usually HTML tags, such as headings and buttons.

Typography

The headings and paragraph styles here are applied to text throughout the platform. This includes within other molecules/organisms, and within content fields such as article pages.
